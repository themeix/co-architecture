CO-architecture

Our profession is profoundly connected to land, people and place.

CO-architecture was founded in Boorloo on Whadjik Noongar Country and we are proud to build our community across Australia. In doing so we pay our respects to our First Nations people, the Traditional Custodians of the land. We acknowledge the Elders, past, present and emerging, and the continuation of cultural, spiritual and educational practices of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples.
